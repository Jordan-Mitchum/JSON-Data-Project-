The data used in this web app is from the CORGIS Dataset Project and can be found here. The data contain the information of the Superbowl ads. It has data from the years 2000-2020.
The data set was created by a group of people named FiveThirtyEight who went through and analyzed and categorized the ads and then definded them in based on characteristics and criteria.
One bit of information used reports for each ad is the content it used. The content used is shown in the data set by its describing fetures.
For example, if an ad used comedy or other typs of way to appeal to the audience. Each way of entertainment is put into its own subcategroy. This just shows a little of what the data set shows.
